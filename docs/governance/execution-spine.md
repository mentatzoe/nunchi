# Retired SpecKit execution spine

The SpecKit workflow and slice lifecycle were retired on 2026-07-24 because
they encouraged work about delivery to substitute for delivery itself.

The product decisions, detailed specifications, technical plans, acceptance
scenes, ownership, dependency order, and historical evidence remain available.
They are implemented through ordinary product branches and pull requests using
[`docs/v2-delivery.md`](../v2-delivery.md).

Historical lifecycle records under `evidence/` remain provenance only. They do
not authorize work, determine current status, or establish product completion.
